What Relic is

A statue. A memory. A whole game.

SLA-printed resin, ten centimetres, themed to the title it holds. In its base, a USB flash memory carrying the entire game. Plug it in and play. Nothing sits in between.

No installation required

The app boots from the statue itself. Prefer to copy everything to disk? The files are right there, in the clear.

The statue is the license

The chip has a unique identity. The key is derived locally on your hardware — no servers, no accounts.

Real preservation

Game files stay visible and copyable. No walled store, no middleman deciding what survives.

Two app layers

Lightweight inside the statue. Full on the system.

You don't need to install anything to play. When you own more than one, a companion app keeps the collection in order.

Embedded · inside the Relic

Ember

Lightweight app living in the Flux partition. It boots straight from the statue, no installation. Handles authentication, mounting Core, launcher, DLC.

  • Local authentication via chip UID
  • No account, no server
  • Detects the full companion app if present
  • Zero-install, zero-friction
Companion · on the system

Forge

Full app installable on desktop. Manages your whole collection: library of all your Relics, DLC, signed updates.

  • Multi-Relic library
  • DLC manager
  • Signed updates
  • Suggested by Ember on first launch
